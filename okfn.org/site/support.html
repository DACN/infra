Supporting the Open Knowledge Foundation

You can support the Foundation in a variety of ways from donating money to donating your time. You may wish to take a look at a summary of the Foundation's activities or its projects to get an idea what your contribution would be used for and if you wish you can specify that your contribution go to a particular area of the Foundation's work.

Donating Time

One of the biggest ways you can support the Foundation is by volunteering your time and effort. If you would like to get involved please mail info [at] okfn.org with volunteer in the subject line or sign up on the volunteers page of the wiki.

Donating Money

We can receive payments via three methods. If you wish your money to be used for a particular purpose or for a particular area of the Foundation's work please specify that when you make your payment or email info [at] okfn.org.

Donating Resources

An alternative to donating money is to donate resources such as equipment, hosting, printing etc. If you want to make such a donation please mail us at info [at] okfn.org with the details.
