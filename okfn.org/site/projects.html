The Network of Projects

Introduction

The Open Knowledge Foundation is organized around a network of individual projects, each focused on a different aspect of open knowledge, but united by a common set of concerns, and a commmon set of traditions in both etiquette and process.

Each project is delegated authority over its activities, and is given a great deal of latitude in both what it does and how it does it. A central Board is responsible for providing infrastructure as well as monitoring and mentoring projects (the governance document provides full details).
